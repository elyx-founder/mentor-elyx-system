ABOUT ELYX

ELYX is an AI-powered accountability mentor that gives you daily missions tailored to your goal, tracks your progress with gamified coins and levels, and holds you accountable like a real coach.

🔒 Privacy: All your data (name, goal, progress) stays on your device in localStorage. Nothing is sent to any server except your chat messages to the AI.

👨‍💻 Built by an indie founder. Open source on GitHub.

Found a bug or have feedback? Open an issue on GitHub or message on Twitter.
